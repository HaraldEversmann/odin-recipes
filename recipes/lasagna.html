Lasagna recipe

Description

The best Italian dish ever -Hell, the best dish ever!

Ingredients

  • Pasta dough
  • meat
  • tomatoes
  • sauce

Steps

  1. Cook meat and tomatoes together. This is your filling.
  2. Build the lasagna by starting out with a layer of filling, then a layer of pasta, and then filling again. You should end with a layer of pasta?
  3. Add sauce on top, along with some cheese if you like.
  4. Put the lasagna in the oven for 40 minutes @ 5000degree F.
